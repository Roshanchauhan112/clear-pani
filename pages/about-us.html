About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind CLEAR Water Bottles

Our Story

Founded in 2010, CLEAR Water Bottles began with a simple mission: to create premium water bottles that combine style, functionality, and environmental responsibility.

What started as a small workshop in Mumbai has grown into a nationwide brand with a presence in 18 states across India. Our journey has been defined by innovation, quality, and a deep commitment to sustainability.

Today, CLEAR is recognized as a leader in the premium water bottle industry, offering a wide range of products that help people stay hydrated while reducing plastic waste.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality hydration solutions while reducing plastic waste and promoting a healthier, more sustainable lifestyle for our customers.

Quality

We are committed to excellence in every bottle we produce, using only the finest materials and rigorous quality control.

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility is at the core of our business. We design products that help reduce single-use plastic waste.

Innovation

We continuously strive to improve our products through research, design, and technological advancement.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers, employees, and the communities where we operate.

Our Journey

2010

CLEAR Water Bottles founded in Mumbai with a small team of 5 people.

2012

Launched our first line of premium glass water bottles.

2014

Expanded operations to 5 states across India.

2016

Received ISO certification for quality management systems.

2018

Launched eco-friendly packaging initiative, reducing plastic waste by 75%.

2020

Celebrated 10 years with presence in 15 states and over 500 employees.

2023

Expanded to 18 states with 1600+ employees and launched international shipping.
